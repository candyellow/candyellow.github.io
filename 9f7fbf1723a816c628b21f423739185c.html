My most favorite workout! The Club Tryouts

Cora / 06.04.2021

My most favorite workout! The Club Tryouts Summer isn't over yet, but it is already late. It is already time to get rid of the winter blues. It is already time to work on your feet, toss and turn, and change your usual activity into something new and creative. Exercise has its own charm, a unique way of expressing itself. Choose a few of these as your daily workout routine: first, some aerobic exercises, such as walking, running, biking, or skating. Then, some resistance exercises, such as Boxing, Thai Boxing, mma techniques will make your feet feel good. And if you decide to go for Boxing, then Boxing will be the best option for you. Boxing training should take anywhere from 45 minutes to 1 hour. The maximum time for which you can train in a week is 1 hour 45 minutes. You can train as many as 8-10 times a week. The rest time between training sessions is up to 45 minutes. Your feet should be constantly in motion. this period of time will be called the warm-up. The first thing you should do is warm up before training. This is important. The longer a person spends in the gym, the more muscles they have to pump up. If you want your feet to be in good shape, then you need to start doing exercises right away. sit down 5-10 minutes before a workout and rest 90 seconds.so that you can perform at least 2-3 sets with fully developed muscles. At the same time, you will not be distracted from the correct technique of the exercise. After warming up, you can pump your feet off the floor with a combination of strength and aerobic exercises. Or you can do leg lifts to the ceiling, doing as many as 6 lifts at a time. whichever option is easier for you.
